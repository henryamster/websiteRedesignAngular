About Me


I grew up in Richmond, Kentucky, near the historic Fort Boonesboro State Park and attended Madison Central High School, where I was an officer in the Junior Classical League (Latin Club), participated in the Academic Team all 4 years and was a participant in the National Youth Leadership Foundation Conference for Technology in San Diego in 2004. I spent most of my free time writing, playing, and recording music with friends, playing World of Warcraft, and trying to impress girls with my myspace profile designs ðŸ˜†

After graduating high school, I moved to Lexington, Kentucky, to attend the University of Kentucky, which I did on and off over the next five years. I was a volunteer DJ at WRFL and played in a couple of different bands during this time. I put my education on hold to take a management job at Meijer for financial security, where I had been working while going to school, and eventually decided to step down from my management position to take a stab at a career in music. I moved to Louisville, Kentucky, in 2014, but ended up deciding being a musician was not a very viable way to build up a retirement fund, and have stuck to it as a hobby ever since.

I moved back to Richmond, Kentucky, in 2016 and worked as an Amazon Distribution Facility Picker for one year, and as a produce clerk at Meijer for the years in between moving back and finishing my degree at Eastern Kentucky University, where I enrolled in 2017. I studied Computer Information Systems at Eastern, and after graduating, took an internship with Meijer in Grand Rapids, Michigan, where I worked with the Cloud Infrastructure team.

After declining an offer to work so far away from home, I ended up taking a position with EKU's Training Resource Center, where I was hired as a Javascript Developer, but have since taken on many different and interesting responsibilities.

I am not married, I don't have any pets, but I do live with my entire family in a (somewhat) remote cabin in the woods. We're not preppers or anything! It's just so nice to not breathe in the city smog and have to deal with ambulance sirens, noisy neighbors, et c. When I'm not working, making music or art, or otherwise working behind a computer, you can find me shooting hoops out on the front lawn, trying to do pull-ups on my pull-up bar, absorbing sunlight on the back deck, going to friend's concerts, drawing, and watching Hulu, HBO, or YouTube videos.
